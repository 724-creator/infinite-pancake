Understanding the Frameworks

Stars are categorized simultaneously using two primary methodologies:

  • Harvard Classification: Alphabetical classes (O, B, A, F, G, K, M) mapping surface temperature from hottest to coolest.
  • Yerkes (MK) Classification: Roman numerals (I-V) tracking atmospheric density, directly identifying the star's evolutionary stage (Giants vs Dwarfs).
